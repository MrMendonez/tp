What we do:



Any ad we work on guarantees viewers that the person portrayed in our ad is free of any photoshop/retouching enhancements.

Our name, "Trusting Pixels" stands for exactly what we do. We help bring a little bit more truth in today's advertisements by authenticating ads that leave people as they are. We are a mark that lets people know that no post production enhancements were done to the people portrayed in an ad.

This company was built for people (consumers). Its quite evident that over the years people have wanted to see a little more truth in advertisements, so we created this company to help authenticate ads that not only promote brands, but ads that promote real people as well.
