Why we do it:

People should be able to differentiate from what is false and what is real.

The advertising world has become too comfortable with the capabilities of what software can do, and in return neglecting what is being put out into the world and the negative effect it can have on people. Today's youth are growing up in an era that has them completely surrounded by media advertising a false representation of people. The issue is not one retouched ad, but its the thousands seen everyday that become a representation of what people look like. Trusting Pixels is the first platform that allow viewers to know what they're looking at.
