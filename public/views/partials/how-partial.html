How we do it:


We work with our clients from the very start of their production, gathering the original images captured on set. We then make sure that our clients stay in line with our regulations by taking their final image and comparing it to the original that was captured on set to make sure no enhancements were made in accordance to our regulations.

After analyzing and approving the content, we then promote the ad across our social media platforms and on our website. As stated per our partnership agreement, our clients can also promote their advertisement as long they no longer enhance it since our name is tied in partnership with Trusting Pixels Inc.
